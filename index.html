Morfis

AI-Assisted Physical Product Customization

Dimitrios Mallis, Anis Kacem, Djamila Aouada.
SnT, University of Luxembourg

Overview

CAD design traditionally requires specialized skills and advanced technical knowledge, restricting the design process to professional engineers and designers. Nevertheless, many industries could benefit from offering robust product customisation features. Current solutions typically focus on specific object types and allow only limited modifications. Our goal is to enable users to interact with designs through both text and drawing, harnessing AI-Assisted CAD. By doing so, we aim to democratize product design and empower diverse customers to create products that precisely match their needs, requirements, and aesthetic preferences.
