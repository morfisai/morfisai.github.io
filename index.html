Overview

CAD design traditionally requires specialized skills and advanced technical knowledge, restricting the design process to professional engineers and designers. Morfis is a software platform that allows users to interact with complex 3D CAD models using natural language, enabling them to effortlessly modify, add, or remove its parts. Additionally, it supports the iterative creation of CAD designs from scratch based on textual descriptions, making CAD more accessible to non-experts by eliminating the need for complex software interfaces. The Morfis project, aims to democratize product design and empower diverse customers to create products that precisely match their needs, requirements, and aesthetic preferences.
